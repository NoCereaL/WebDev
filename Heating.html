Boiler Servicing

Regular boiler servicing is vital to ensure that your boiler is running safely and efficiently. Poorly maintained or faulty boilers present a serious health risk to you and your family because they can cause fatal carbon monoxide poisoning.

Arrange a British Gas boiler service with Plumbing Heating Air Conditioning Services at least once a year to ensure your boiler is operating properly at all times. History has proven that most people choose to have their boiler serviced in winter when it is used the most. You may want to take this into account when booking your appointment with us – it may be worthwhile booking your service in advance or in a different season, such as summer.

Boiler Repairs

If your boiler servicing shows any shortcomings of your boiler, or if it simply doesn’t work, PHACS Gas Engineers can also repair your boiler to ensure it is running at optimum efficiency.

 

Boiler Installation

PHACS can offer you expert boiler installation. Whether you need to replace your old boiler or have one fitted in a new building, our certified Gas-Safe engineers can have your state-of-the-art boiler up and running in your commercial or residential property.

 
Radiator Installation

If your radiators are beyond saving or if you simply want to install upgrades, PHACS can help. We have experience installing radiators in commercial and residential areas, meeting the demands of any heating requirements. We can install anything from standard to designer radiators

 

Power Flushing

PHACS  has the objective is to restore systems with circulation and boiler noise problems (caused by sludge and corrosion deposits) to optimum operation. Power flushing removes these deposits and the problems that they cause. We know that it is prudent to power flush a heating system immediately before fitting a new boiler to an existing system to prevent possible future problems. In our years of experience, many systems are found to contain corrosion and sludge, even if no flow problems have yet shown themselves. So why not get in touch with experts to help you solve your problem.
